Yasinskyi
Geometry Olympiad

The Yasinskyi Geometry Olympiad is an annual online competition that brings together young mathematical talents to explore the beauty and rigor of geometry. Named in honor of Viacheslav Yasinskyi, a legendary Ukrainian mathematics teacher and a master of composing challenging and inspiring problems, this Olympiad is a celebration of mathematical excellence.

Viacheslav Yasinskyi

Competition Details

The Olympiad provides an excellent opportunity for students to test their skills, compete with peers from around the world, and learn from the finest minds in mathematics. The difficulty of the proposed problems corresponds to the level of national competitions. Stay tuned for announcements, problem sets, results, and more. We look forward to welcoming you to this exciting event!

When The next olympiad will be held on December 15, 2024.
Who Can Participate Students in grades 8, 9 and 10-11
Format Online
Rules Solve 5 geometry problems in 4 hours

Current Olympiad 2024

Welcome to the main page for the 2024 Yasinskyi Geometry Olympiad. You’ll find all the essential information and links to participate in this year’s competition.

Problems

The problems for the Olympiad will be available here at 11:00 GMT+2, December 15, 2024. Please check back at that time to download the PDF and begin solving the challenges!

Registration

On the day of the competition, a Submission Form will appear on this page. Participants must fill out the form and attach their solutions in PDF format before the deadline.

Registration Form will contain the next fields

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Country
  • City
  • School
  • Grade
The submission form will close at 15:15, December 15, 2024.

Results

The results of the competition will be published here after careful evaluation of the submissions. Stay tuned!

Contact Us

The Yasinskyi Geometry Olympiad invites mathematicians, educators, and problem enthusiasts to collaborate with us in shaping this competition into a world-class event.

For any questions regarding the competition, participation, or organizational matters, feel free to reach out to us at email: olimpiada.yas@gmail.com

We are always seeking original and challenging geometry problems for future Olympiads. If you are passionate about creating mathematical problems that inspire and test the skills of young minds, we encourage you to contribute.
